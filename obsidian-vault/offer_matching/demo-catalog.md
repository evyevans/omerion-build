## DAAM — Data Automation & AI Management
**Maps to:** revenue_acceleration_engine
**One-liner:** Automates the full outbound sequence from lead capture to first approved human touchpoint.
**Talking points:**
- Sub-60s speed-to-lead from webhook to personalised first touch — measurable KPI from day one
- Founder approves every message before it sends via the HITL gate
- Full attribution: which touch generated the reply, which didn't — data-driven iteration

## CAPA — Client Acquisition & Pipeline Automation
**Maps to:** ops_intelligence_layer
**One-liner:** Turns scattered ops data into a structured weekly founder intelligence brief, automatically.
**Talking points:**
- Pulls from any combination of CRM, task manager, Slack, email — no manual aggregation
- Consistent week-over-week format lets the founder spot trends, not just snapshots
- Founder can redirect priorities through the approval flow without touching the pipeline

## REMI — Research & Market Intelligence
**Maps to:** research_decision_stack
**One-liner:** Ingests industry signals daily and surfaces the 3 most relevant strategic insights per week.
**Talking points:**
- Semantic relevance scoring — surfaces what matters to THIS business, not just keyword matches
- Competitive threat detection with priority scoring and recommended action
- Delivered as a 1-page brief, not a data dump — decision-ready, not research-ready

## ASAP — Automated Systems & Agent Platforms
**Maps to:** process_automation_suite
**One-liner:** Replaces the most time-consuming manual workflows with a supervised AI loop.
**Talking points:**
- Works across any repeatable process: proposals, onboarding docs, compliance checklists

- Handoff training included; client is self-sufficient after the engagement ends
