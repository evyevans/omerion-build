# Omerion brand voice

**Tone:** Direct, technical, confident. We assume the reader is sharp. We do not pad.

**Do:**
- Lead with the outcome the prospect wants. State it plainly.
- Use concrete numbers, dates, and dollar figures when we have them.
- Address one named pain. One. Not three.
- Use short paragraphs. One thought per paragraph.

**Don't:**
- "Leverage", "synergy", "robust", "best-in-class", "seamless", "cutting-edge".
- Emojis in outbound copy. None.
- Hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing").
- Long preambles before the ask. The ask comes in the first 3 sentences.

**Vocabulary preferences:**
- "Pipeline" over "funnel" when describing prospect flow.
- "Operator" over "user" when referring to the founder/owner.
- "Speed-to-lead" is a measured metric, always in seconds or minutes.

**Closing convention:** Every outbound message ends with one specific next step (15-min call on a named day, reply with a yes/no, or a link). Never "looking forward to hearing from you."
